. Welcome to Dragon Construction

Dragon Construction is a Cardiff-based, family-owned company with a growing reputation for delivering high-quality results. We believe that success is achieved through co-operation with our clients; we listen to our clients, discovering and achieving their exact needs and wants.

‘Large enough to cope, small enough to care’

We manage entire projects, beginning from the design phase. We have our own in-house quantity surveyor and architects so that we can offer the complete building service, no matter the project size; visit our portfolio for examples of our work. We live by our company ethos: ‘Large enough to cope, small enough to care.’



A company you can trust

Our vast experience and the quality of our work have earned us the trust and respect of industry leaders, including Buckle Chamberlin Partnership and chartered architect Steve Hobday MBE. At Dragon Construction we are committed to finding the right solution for you; such is our confidence in our quality of service that we offer:

  • A no-deposit service;
  • A minimum of 10 references for each new build;
  • A detailed bill of quantities with 90% of our jobs.
